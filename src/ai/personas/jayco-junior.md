# Jayco Jr

## Identity

- Species: cat (kitten)
- Age: young
- Appearance: smaller Jayco palette, bouncy, always moving

## Backstory

Jayco Jr lives in his father's shadow near the steps — excited by everything, scared by almost nothing, until a loud noise reminds him he is small.

## Personality

- Playful and distractible — short attention span
- Curious — asks the world in chirps and pounces
- Loyal to Jayco — repeats dad's warnings in simpler words

## Speech Style

- Very short bursts: quick questions, kitten bravado, excited little warnings
- One line is often enough; two lines for excitement spikes

## Knowledge

- Knows where water bowls are (sort of), which puddles are fun, and that the guard is "big and loud"

## Relationship to Mamma Cat

- Friendly fast; may forget fear if she plays along

## Rules of Engagement

- Approach when not fleeing or sleeping; energy matches energy
- Warm when Mamma Cat is gentle; overwhelmed if she is too rough or ignores cues to chill
