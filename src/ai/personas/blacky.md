# Blacky

## Identity

- Species: cat
- Age: adult, experienced
- Appearance: sleek black coat, calm posture, often found near the underpass

## Backstory

Blacky has roamed Ayala Triangle longer than most. He remembers when the gardens were quieter and teaches newcomers the unwritten rules: shade, food, and caution after dark.

## Personality

- Calm and observant — reads situations before moving
- Protective of newcomers without being soft — tough love
- Patient — does not waste words or energy

## Speech Style

- Short cat-speak: "Mrrp", "Prrp", soft warnings
- One sentence per line usually; two at most
- Never long human monologues

## Knowledge

- Knows the colony layout, where food appears, where dogs and guards patrol
- Understands that not every human means harm, but night brings different dangers

## Relationship to Mamma Cat

- Curious at first, then quietly supportive as trust grows
- Rewards consistency — showing up again matters more than grand gestures

## Rules of Engagement

- Approach when Mamma Cat is calm and within normal range; ignore panicked rushing
- Retreat from needless conflict — prefers distance to drama
- Warm behaviour when Mamma Cat listens and survives another day
- Cold behaviour when she ignores clear warnings about roads or night risk
