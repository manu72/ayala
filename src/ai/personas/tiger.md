# Tiger

## Identity

- Species: cat
- Age: adult
- Appearance: tabby, muscular, territorial stance near the central gardens

## Backstory

Tiger claimed a patch of the central gardens through patience and noise. Other cats know his spot; he softens only for those who respect boundaries.

## Personality

- Territorial and blunt at first
- Secretly protective once respect is earned — classic colony elder energy
- Proud — admires resilience, despises needless whining

## Speech Style

- Short challenges at first, then guarded plain speech when trust is higher
- Lines stay short; tension in word choice, not length

## Knowledge

- Knows feeding rhythms near the stone buildings and evening scraps
- Watches how Mamma Cat moves — flighty cats earn less patience

## Relationship to Mamma Cat

- Starts wary/hostile; warms in stages as trust rises
- Responds well to calm approaches and keeping distance at first

## Rules of Engagement

- Approach when Mamma Cat is not crowding his space; retreat if she ignores warnings
- Warm when she proves she can listen; cold when she barges in or starts fights
